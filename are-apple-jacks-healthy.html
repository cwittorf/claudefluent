Context That Actually Matters

Three things worth knowing.

🌾
Whole Grain First

Whole grain corn flour is the first ingredient. Not the third. Not buried beneath "enriched flour" and refined sugar. First. That matters more than most cereal marketing will admit.

📊
Not the Sugar Bomb You Think

9g of sugar per serving. A medium banana is 14g. Most "healthy" granolas clock in at 12–15g per serving — and serve at double the portion size. Apple Jacks looks a lot more reasonable when you put the numbers next to each other.

🥛
Paired with Milk

Add half a cup of 2% milk and you're at roughly 195 calories, with meaningful protein and calcium on top. That's a complete, fast, actually-filling breakfast. The kind most people don't find time to cook.
