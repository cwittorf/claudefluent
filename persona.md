# Target Buyer Persona

## Name: Alex

**Age:** 30
**Role:** Apple Jacks Enthusiast

---

## About

Alex is a 30-year-old adult who grew up eating Apple Jacks and never outgrew the love for it. Whether it's a quick breakfast before work or a late-night snack, Apple Jacks is a staple in Alex's kitchen. The familiar orange and green loops and that distinct cinnamon-apple flavor bring a sense of comfort and nostalgia.

---

## Traits

- Nostalgic — has fond childhood memories tied to Apple Jacks
- Busy lifestyle — values quick, easy, and satisfying meal options
- Young adult mindset — fun-loving, not afraid to enjoy "kid" cereals as an adult
- Loyal brand consumer — sticks to favorites and resists switching

---

## Goals

- Start the day with something fast, tasty, and familiar
- Relive a bit of childhood joy in everyday moments
- Find products that fit into a busy adult routine without sacrificing fun

---

## Pain Points

- Cereals that are too sugary or lack flavor complexity
- Packaging that feels too childish or too "health-focused" — wants balance
- Running out of Apple Jacks unexpectedly

---

## Buying Behavior

- Purchases Apple Jacks regularly as a pantry staple
- Often buys in bulk or family-size boxes for value
- Influenced by nostalgia, taste, and brand familiarity over health claims
